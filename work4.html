ICT in Teaching and Learning

This module introduces students to the use of information and communication technologies in modern education. It focuses on how digital tools can support teaching, enhance student engagement, and improve learning outcomes. Throughout the module, students explore ICT applications for presenting new material, organizing interactive tasks, assessing learning, and supporting differentiated and distance instruction. The course also addresses the benefits and challenges of ICT use in schools and develops the digital competence teachers need to create effective, technology-rich learning environments.
