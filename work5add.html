Test 1

It’s a 10-question online quiz designed to help you discover your personal leadership style. The test is meant for a broad audience — not only business managers, but also teachers, trainers, and anyone who leads or guides others. It’s based on several common leadership styles (such as democratic, autocratic, laissez-faire, and transformational) and helps you understand which one best fits your personality and approach. At the end, you receive your dominant leadership type along with practical tips on how to build on your strengths and improve weaker areas.

Test 2

It’s a fun, short online quiz created by the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services as part of the We Think Twice youth initiative. The quiz is specifically designed for teens to help them discover their personal leadership style and understand how they can make a positive impact in their school or community. It includes simple, relatable questions about teamwork, communication, and decision-making in everyday school situations. After finishing, students receive one of several leadership profiles (for example: The Visionary, The Collaborator, The Motivator), along with tips on how to use their strengths to lead effectively.
